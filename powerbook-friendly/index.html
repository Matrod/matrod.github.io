Powerbook Friendly

March 3, 2005 by daemon

I´m the proud owner of an Apple iBook notebook. I´m quite happy with it, but at the same time I´m tempted to change it for one of the new breed of Powerbooks, since they have reduced their price in a very interesting way. This can sound a little dumb, but I need more power for my applications (Reason, Photoshop, etc.) and I don´t want to spend US$ 2500 in a G5 dual processor computer (It could be really nice, but money is always an issue). My main problem? I live in a spanish-speaking country and my iBook has an english keyboard layout. I´m going to offer it through eBay to see what happens. The worst case scenario would be to have two laptops, and using only one… I´m confident that someone will help me out.
