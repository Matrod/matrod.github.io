Fortunate Lives

February 28, 2005 by daemon

Look at you for a moment. What do you see? I think I know the answer: you probably punish yourself too hard. “I see worries, that I’m ugly/fat/tall/short, problems, not enough money”;. Have you stopped only once to think that you are surrounded by people with lots of trouble, sickness, that do drugs, smoke, trapped in alcoholism, workoholic, with very dark and dramatic stories behind, and they do continue to run their lives in the way they can? I don’t think so. Many of us keep complaining about that our lives are too boring or flat, but this is not true. We are really lucky, but (this is like a noir-humour joke) almost always we need that life hits us hard to realize that. Stop being stupid and start living your life without waiting for “that” to happen. Remember, tomorrow never comes…
