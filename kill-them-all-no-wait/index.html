Kill them all! (no, wait!)

March 31, 2005 by daemon

Human beings don´t stop to amaze me (but not in the best way, I must admit), specially when I realize that many of us simply don´t show any trace of respect or “fair behavior” with each other… I deeply hate the people that, for example, park their cars using two or more (!) parking places. Parking is getting more and more difficult here in Europe these days, (I´m starting to believe that buying a car is the people´s ultimate goal in their lives, or something), and those morons only think in themselves. It does not represent an extra effort to spend a little more time parking in the appropriate way, just to allow other persons to have a suitable place for their cars, too… But when I´m about to act and try to cope with this, I find projects like the Guedelon Castle that reminds me about that not everyone in this planet behaves badly…
