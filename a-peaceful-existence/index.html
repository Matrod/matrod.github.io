A peaceful existence

December 1, 2013 by daemon

Some time ago when I read his story about a marriage ending, I couldn’t help but wonder what was the sensation he was talking about like. It’s been almost three months since we decided to stop walking together and I must say I’ve regained a long lost feeling of peace, solitude, calmness and most important, my time is mine again.

Days come and go and I’m happy, healthy and learning gradually not to worry about small things. The rush is finally over, at least for a while. Chopin, some good Madeira wine and a silent home along with my cat is all I want. Happiness doesn’t have to be expensive or complicated at all.
